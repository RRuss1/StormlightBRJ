# Chapter 1: Character Creation

## Before Character Creation

Before filling out a character sheet, **imagine who you want to play**. This can be as specific as "a listener keeper of songs" or as simple as "a tough mercenary." You can come up with this alone or collaborate with the group.

Consider:
- What story feels important or healing to tell?
- How does your character mesh with the campaign's theme?
- What challenges might they face, and how might they grow?

---

## Character Creation Steps

1. Choose your origins
2. Choose your starting path
3. Choose your attributes
4. Choose your skills and expertises
5. Choose your talents
6. Equip yourself
7. Tell your story
8. Calculate your final statistics

---

## Step 1: Origins

### Ancestry
Choose from two sapient species on Roshar:
- **Human** — the majority species across Roshar's nations
- **Singer** — the indigenous people of Roshar; can change forms by bonding spren

Your ancestry guides your appearance and may unlock unique talents.

**On Your Character Sheet:** Record ancestry name and special rules. You'll choose ancestry bonus talents in Step 5.

### Culture
Choose **up to two cultural expertises** from Chapter 2. These represent the societies that shaped you — granting language fluency, cultural knowledge, and worldview.

---

## Step 2: Starting Path

Choose your **heroic path** — your first source of specialized abilities.

| Path | Theme | Starting Skill | Key Talent |
|------|-------|---------------|------------|
| **Agent** | Talented operative with a keen mind or deft hand | Insight | Opportunist: Reroll the plot die once per round |
| **Envoy** | Insightful negotiator who influences others | Discipline | Rousing Presence: Make an ally Determined |
| **Hunter** | Sharpshooter and outdoorsperson | Perception | Seek Quarry: Gain advantage against a chosen target |
| **Leader** | Commander who guides others | Leadership | Decisive Command: Add a d4 command die to an ally's tests |
| **Scholar** | Adroit thinker who excels at planning | Lore | Erudition: Gain bonus skill ranks to reallocate |
| **Warrior** | Fighter relying on skill, strength, or will | Athletics | Vigilant Stance: A fighting stance that makes you responsive |

**On Your Character Sheet:** Record your starting path name. You'll add the starting skill in Step 4 and a talent in Step 5.

> **How Do I Create a Radiant?**
> New characters aren't Radiant at creation (unless the GM decides otherwise). To follow a Radiant path, your character must first swear Ideals during play (see Chapter 5).

---

## Step 3: Attributes

Distribute **12 points** across the six attributes. Maximum of **3 in any one attribute** at creation.

| Attribute | Governs |
|-----------|---------|
| **Strength** | Physical power, lifting capacity, health |
| **Speed** | Quickness, movement rate |
| **Intellect** | Intelligence, expertises, cognitive skills |
| **Willpower** | Mental fortitude, focus, recovery die |
| **Awareness** | Wisdom, senses range, spiritual skills |
| **Presence** | Charisma, connections, spiritual skills |

**Derived Statistics to Record:**
- **Lifting Capacity** (from Strength table)
- **Movement Rate** (from Speed table)
- **Recovery Die** (from Willpower table)
- **Senses Range** (from Awareness table)

---

## Step 4: Skills and Expertises

### Gain a Starting Skill Rank
Your starting path grants **1 free rank** in its associated skill.

### Choose Additional Skill Ranks
Distribute **4 more ranks** among the 18 skills. Maximum 2 ranks at creation.

**Skill Modifier = attribute score + skill ranks + bonuses/penalties**

### Choose Expertises
- You already have **2 cultural expertises** from Step 1.
- If your **Intellect is 1+**, choose additional expertises equal to your Intellect score.

---

## Step 5: Talents

### Key Talent
You automatically gain the **first talent** in your starting path's talent tree.

### Ancestry Bonus Talent
- **Singers:** Gain *Change Form* plus one form talent.
- **Humans:** Gain one additional talent from any heroic path.

---

## Step 6: Equipment

Choose a **starting kit** from Chapter 7:

| Kit | Highlights |
|-----|-----------|
| **Academic** | Knife/sling/staff, uniform, books, 3d12 marks; gains Literature expertise |
| **Artisan** | Light weapon, leather armor, instruments, 4d8 marks |
| **Courtier** | Sword or longbow, fine clothing, 4d20 marks; grants a noble patron |
| **Military** | Two weapons, chain armor, rations, 2d6 marks |
| **Prisoner** | No gear; gains a connection to a Radiant spren (2 milestones toward First Ideal) |
| **Underworld** | Two light weapons, leather armor, thieves' tools, 1d20 marks |

**Record Your Deflect Value:** Based on armor worn (0 if none).

---

## Step 7: Story

### Purpose
Your **core drive** — what you can't help but act on. Examples:
- Save Others
- Seek Truth
- Find Connection
- Seek Justice
- Preserve Life

### Obstacle
What **persistently gets in your way**. Examples:
- Deep Regret
- Spite
- Self-Doubt
- Mistrust of Others

### Goals
Choose **1–2 starting goals** — concrete objectives with three milestone checkboxes. When all three are checked and the goal is concluded, you earn a **reward**.

### Tell Your Story
Fill in details: occupation, relationships, loyalties, personality, appearance, and possibly a Radiant order you aspire to.

---

## Step 8: Final Calculations

### Health
> **Maximum Health = 10 + Strength + bonuses/penalties**

### Focus
> **Maximum Focus = 2 + Willpower + bonuses/penalties**

### Investiture
Leave blank for now — unlocked when you choose a Radiant path's First Ideal talent.

### Defenses

| Defense | Formula |
|---------|---------|
| **Physical** | 10 + Strength + Speed + bonuses |
| **Cognitive** | 10 + Intellect + Willpower + bonuses |
| **Spiritual** | 10 + Awareness + Presence + bonuses |

### Other Fields
- **Level:** Record as 1.
- **Conditions & Injuries:** Leave blank for now.

---

## Character Advancement

Characters advance in two ways:
1. **Gaining levels** (from GM-declared milestones)
2. **Completing goals** (for powerful rewards)

### Gaining a Level
1. Increase character level by 1
2. At levels 3, 6, 9, 12, 15, 18 — increase one attribute
3. Increase health
4. Gain 2 skill ranks
5. Gain 1 talent (plus ancestry bonus talents at tier starts: levels 1, 6, 11, 16, 21)

### Character Advancement Table (Summary)

| Tier | Levels | Attribute Points | Health Gained | Max Skill Rank | Skill Ranks | Talents |
|------|--------|-----------------|---------------|----------------|-------------|---------|
| 1 | 1–5 | 12 at start; +1 at lvl 3 | 10+STR at lvl 1; +5/level | 2 | 4 at creation; +2/level | Key + ancestry at lvl 1; +1/level |
| 2 | 6–10 | +1 at lvls 6, 9 | +4+STR at lvl 6; +4/level | 3 | +2/level | +1/level; ancestry at lvl 6 |
| 3 | 11–15 | +1 at lvls 12, 15 | +3+STR at lvl 11; +3/level | 4 | +2/level | +1/level; ancestry at lvl 11 |
| 4 | 16–20 | +1 at lvl 18 | +2+STR at lvl 16; +2/level | 5 | +2/level | +1/level; ancestry at lvl 16 |
| 5 | 21+ | — | +1/level | 5 | +1 OR +1 talent | Ancestry at lvl 21 |

### Prerequisites
Some talents require specific skill ranks, sworn Ideals, or narrative achievements. Temporary bonuses don't count toward prerequisites.

### Choosing Rewards
- Radiant characters spend most rewards on spren bond advancement and Ideals.
- Heroic (non-Radiant) characters tend to build powerful connections, assets, and titles.
