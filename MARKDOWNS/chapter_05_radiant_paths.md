# Chapter 5: Radiant Paths

## Using Radiant Paths

Radiant paths operate like heroic paths but carry far deeper narrative weight. Becoming a Knight Radiant means bonding a sapient spren, swearing oaths, and committing to a set of **Ideals** for life.

Each Radiant path grants three talent trees:
1. **Spren Bond tree** (presented in this chapter)
2. **Two Surge trees** (presented in Chapter 6)

---

## Investiture and Stormlight

**Investiture** is the energy that suffuses the cosmere. On Roshar, it manifests as **Stormlight**.

### Spending Investiture
- Fuels surges, surge talents, and spren bond talents
- Maintains ongoing **Invested effects** (infusions)
- If current Investiture drops to 0, you can't maintain ongoing effects beyond their remaining durations

### Regaining Stormlight
- Radiants breathe Stormlight from **infused spheres** within 5 ft.
- **Breathe Stormlight (▶):** Draw from infused spheres; recover Investiture to maximum
- Assume you have enough if you carry marks worth 3× your Investiture total and highstorms occur regularly
- You start most scenes at full Investiture (assumed pre-scene breathing); if **Surprised**, start with 1 Investiture only

### Stormlight Actions
Gained when you choose a First Ideal talent:

| Action | Cost | Effect |
|--------|------|--------|
| **Breathe Stormlight (▶)** | — | Regain Investiture from infused spheres within 5 ft. |
| **Enhance (▶)** | 1 Investiture | Become Enhanced [Strength +1] and Enhanced [Speed +1] until end of next turn; spend 1 Investiture as ▷ each subsequent turn to maintain |
| **Regenerate (▷)** | 1 Investiture | Recover health equal to **1d6 + your current tier** |

> *Breathe Stormlight and Regenerate can be used even while Unconscious.*

## Playing a Radiant

### Before Choosing a Radiant Path
Talk to your GM before you level up. They can introduce a spren to the narrative and provide opportunities to embody your prospective order's Ideals.

### Choosing a First Ideal Talent
- Requires level 2+
- When chosen: immediately gain Investiture access and the goal **"Speak the First Ideal"**
- Does not immediately grant surges — those come when the goal is completed

### Attracting a Spren
Spren are drawn to characters whose behavior aligns with a Radiant order's philosophy. Each order's spren has preferences described in its section. Swearing Ideals requires the spren's acceptance.

---

## Swearing Ideals

### Ideals and Goals
Progress through each Ideal via **Ideal goals** — granted by the corresponding Ideal talent. Goals have **three milestone checkboxes**. Mark a milestone when your actions exemplify the Ideal.

**Speak the First Ideal (all orders):**
> *"Life before death. Strength before weakness. Journey before destination."*

Progress by:
- Prioritizing saving lives over causing death
- Displaying great fortitude amid enormous challenges
- Staying present and focused on people in front of you

### Saying the Words
When all three milestones are checked, coordinate with your GM for a dramatically fitting moment. The GM prompts you to speak the Words; they reply "these Words are accepted."

### Rewards for Speaking an Ideal
Completing an Ideal goal grants:
- Become **Empowered** until end of current scene
- Unlock remaining benefits from that Ideal talent

### Forsaking Your Ideals
- **1st infraction:** Nothing (mistakes happen)
- **2nd infraction (before redeeming yourself):** Lose your highest Ideal and its benefits
- Each continued infraction removes the next highest Ideal
- Enough forsaking can **permanently bar you** from that path and potentially **kill your spren**
- You can repair the bond by proving renewed dedication

## Squires

After speaking the Third Ideal, you can take **squires** — trusted allies who gain a portion of your Radiant powers.

### Squire Ability
While within 50 miles of their Radiant:
- Can draw Stormlight (Investiture maximum 2)
- Can use Breathe Stormlight, Enhance, and Regenerate
- Optionally granted one or both of the Radiant's surges (1 rank each)
- Cannot use the Radiant's talents

Squire status ends if: they choose to leave, the Radiant dismisses them, they become a squire to another, or they bond their own spren.

## Dustbringers

**Philosophy:** *"I will seek self-mastery."*  
**Surges:** Abrasion & Division  
**Spren:** Ashspren

Dustbringers are defined by controlled destruction — precision sappers and mobile artillery who define themselves not by what they destroy, but by what they protect.

### Ideals
- **Second:** *"To control my power, I will control myself."* — Demonstrate restraint, especially around your obstacle.
- **Third:** *"To understand my power, I will understand what power is."* — Witness how political, wealth, physical, and social power function.
- **Fourth:** Acknowledge that all power carries obligation; failure to meet responsibility has consequences.

### Ashspren
- Appear as branching electrical scorches in the Physical Realm
- Deeply hostile to humans due to the Recreance's mass spren deaths
- **Test Assistance:** Advantage on Crafting, setting traps, relevant Lore tests

### Ashspren Bond Talents

**First Ideal (Dustbringer Key)** *(Level 2+)*  
Gain Investiture access. Goal: "Speak the First Ideal." Reward: Empowered; gain Abrasion and Division surge skills (1 rank each).

**Second Ideal** *(Level 4+)*  
Goal: "Speak the Second Ideal." Reward: Empowered; Enhance can be used as ▷ without spending Investiture while you have 1+ Investiture.

**Searing Dust Storm ▶** *(Speak the First Ideal)*  
When you Move along a surface, spend 1+ Investiture to create an obscuring dust cloud in spaces you pass through. For the duration, enemies in the cloud take extra damage (= your Discipline ranks) the first time they take damage each round.

**Invested ∞** *(Searing Dust Storm)*  
Max Investiture increases by your tier; scales with tier.

**Third Ideal** *(Level 8+)*  
Goal: "Speak the Third Ideal." Reward: Empowered; can summon your spren as a **Radiant Shardblade**.

**Deepened Bond ∞** *(Speak the Third Ideal)*  
Spren bond range: 30 ft. → **100 ft.** Giving spren a task costs 1 fewer focus.

**Take Squire (Dustbringer)** *(Speak the Third Ideal)*  
After a long rest, designate a squire. Max squires = your current Ideal number.

**Fourth Ideal** *(Level 13+)*  
Goal: "Speak the Fourth Ideal." Reward: Empowered; can call flamespren as **Radiant Shardplate**.

**Wound Regeneration ▷** *(Invested)*  
When using Regenerate, also spend 2 Investiture to recover from a temporary injury, or 3 Investiture for a permanent one.

> **GM Tip — Canon Accuracy:** In the novels, Dustbringers cannot access Division until the Second Ideal. If your group wants this, add the Second Ideal as a prerequisite to Division and its talents, and replace the First Ideal's Division grant with a Focus Increase.

## Elsecallers

**Surges:** Transformation & Transportation

Elsecallers are the wisest of the Radiants — thoughtful, cautious logisticians and strategists with the strongest connection to the Cognitive Realm.

## Skybreakers

**Philosophy:** *"I will seek justice."*  
**Surges:** Division & Gravitation  
**Spren:** Highspren

Skybreakers are law enforcers — rigid, principled, and devoted to ensuring no one (especially other Radiants) abuses power.

## Truthwatchers

**Philosophy:** *"I will seek truth."*  
**Surges:** Illumination & Progression  
**Spren:** Mistspren (potentially Enlightened)

Truthwatchers are researchers, investigators, and investigative reporters of the Radiant orders.

### Ideals
- **Second:** *"I will seek truth wherever it is hidden."* — Choose fields of truth to seek; begin active investigation.
- **Third:** *"I will reveal truth to all who seek it."* — Share deliberately-withheld knowledge with those who need it most.
- **Fourth:** Acknowledge consequences of sharing forbidden knowledge, then share it anyway — make it impossible to suppress.

### Enlightened Truthwatchers
With GM permission, bond an **Enlightened mistspren** (corrupted by Sja-anat with Voidlight). Gain the **Future Sight** key talent and additional Enlightened talents. These Truthwatchers excel in Spiritual Lightweaving.

### Mistspren
- Appear as humanoid mist with a translucent porcelain mask (Physical Realm: prismatic light shimmer)
- Curious, adventurous, love novelty and new perspectives
- Enlightened mistspren: deep ruby red mist, no mask — glowing red void
- **Test Assistance:** Advantage on Persuasion, approaching new experiences, understanding perspectives, relevant Lore

**Second Ideal** *(Level 4+)*  
Reward: Empowered; Enhance as ▷ without Investiture.

**Spiritual Healing ▶** *(Illumination 2+, Progression 2+, Speak First Ideal)*

**Invested ∞** *(Spiritual Healing)*  
Max Investiture increases by tier; scales.

**Third Ideal** *(Level 8+)*

### Enlightened Talent Tree

**Future Sight (Enlightened Key)** *(Bond an Enlightened mistspren)*  
After a rest or at scene start, ask the GM for a vision of the future. GM may call for a Deduction test; on a Complication, information could be unreliable. Unlocks Enlightened talent tree after speaking the First Ideal.

**Glimpse the Future ★** *(Speak the First Ideal)*  
Once per round after rolling a test, spend 1+ Investiture to reroll that many dice. Must keep final result.

**Search the Past ▶▶** *(Speak the First Ideal)*  
After a long rest, choose a past event. Test Lore (DC scales with how far back: DC 10 for 1 day ago → DC 35 for 10,000 years). On success: experience a 10-minute vision. DC −10 if you witnessed the event directly.

**Alter Fortune ★** *(Glimpse the Future)*  
After each long rest, preroll d20s equal to 1 + tier. When an enemy or ally makes a test, use ↻ to replace their d20 with one of your recorded numbers (after seeing their roll, before effects resolve).

**Vision Void ∞** *(Glimpse or Search)*  
You and allies within spren bond range are immune to enemy effects that replace dice. Enemies cannot view visions of events you influence.

## Playing With Spren

Your spren is both an extension of your character and an individual in their own right.

### Spren Rules
- No stat block; no independent turns
- Moves up to your movement rate per round (ignores terrain penalties)
- Begins at 30-ft bond range (Deepened Bond expands this to 100 ft.)
- Temporarily loses sapience if they move beyond bond range before Deepened Bond

### Interacting With Your Spren
- Generally invisible/inaudible to others (varies by spren type)
- You must speak aloud to communicate (until Third Ideal Shardblade; then telepathic while holding them)
- Giving your spren tasks may cost focus

### Spren Abilities (all orders)
| Ability | Cost | Effect |
|---------|------|--------|
| **Covert Scouting** | 2 focus, several minutes | Spren scouts within bond range; Awareness test may be needed |
| **Oath Encouragement** | 2 focus, ▶ or ↻ | Advantage on next test related to self-doubt, fear, or your obstacle |
| **Sudden Warning** | 3 focus, free | Ignore Surprised or react to undetected threats |
| **Translation** | 2 focus, ▶ | For 1 minute, understand all Rosharan languages; or read one page aloud |

Each spren type also has unique abilities described in their order's section.
