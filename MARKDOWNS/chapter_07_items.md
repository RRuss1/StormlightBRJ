# Chapter 7: Items

## Using Items

### Expert Traits
Some weapons and armor have **expert traits** — benefits that only apply if you have an expertise in that weapon or armor type.

### Charges
Items with charges power special effects. Charges can be recharged during a **short rest** by:
- Draining unencased gemstones (1 charge per gem), or
- Drawing from a Radiant's Investiture (1 charge per Investiture)
- Or fully recharged by leaving it out in a **highstorm**

### Invested Items
Items with Stormlight charges (or otherwise cosmere-connected) are **Invested**. All Shardblades are Invested, as are items charged with Stormlight. Invested items may interact differently with surges.

## Spheres

Roshar's primary currency is the **sphere** — a gemstone encased in a glass bead.

| Size | Description | Diamond Value |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| **Chip** | Tiny sliver | 0.2 marks |
| **Mark** | Half-carat gem | 1 mark |
| **Broam** | Two-carat gem | 4 marks |

### Gemstone Values (in Diamond Marks)

| Gemstone | Chip | Mark | Broam |
|----------|------|------|-------|
| Diamond | 0.2 | 1 | 4 |
| Garnet, heliodor, topaz | 1 | 5 | 20 |
| Ruby, smokestone, zircon | 2 | 10 | 40 |
| Amethyst, sapphire | 5 | 25 | 100 |
| Emerald | 10 | 50 | 200 |

### Using Spheres
- **Infused spheres** glow and can be drawn from by Radiants to fuel Stormlight.
- **Dun spheres** have no Stormlight (but still serve as currency).
- Marks and broams are bright enough to illuminate dark places when infused.

### Wielding Weapons
- Most weapons require **one hand**.
- **Two-Handed** weapons require two hands.
- You can draw a weapon using **Interact (▶)**.
- You can **Drop (▷)** a weapon without an action.
- Your **main hand** weapon attacks as normal.
- Your **offhand** weapon requires spending **2 focus** to Strike with it (1 focus if it has the Offhand trait).
- Only standard Strikes with the offhand require this cost — other actions do not.

---

### Shardblades

- Bonded owner can **summon in 10 heartbeats** (via Interact ▶; materializes start of next turn)
- Can be **dismissed** as a free action
- Up to 5 days to bond; break bond with concentration
- **Radiant Shardblades** (Third Ideal+) summon **immediately** when you use Interact ▶
- Can be reshaped to any melee weapon form (Malleable Form)

**Spiritual Injury Table (used instead of normal Injury table):**

| Roll | Result |
|------|--------|
| 16+ | Flesh Wound (until long rest) |
| 1–15 | Permanent Spiritual Injury (no non-Invested healing) |
| 0 or lower | Death |

> Radiants using non-Radiant Shardblades hear agonized screaming and become Disoriented with disadvantage on all tests; spend 3 focus per turn to suppress.

### Shardplate

**Standard Shardplate Unique Traits:**
- **Charges (4):** Spend 1 charge before taking damage to add +10 deflect (applies to all damage types until attack resolves)
- **Breakable:** GM can spend Opportunity on attacks to deplete 1 charge
- **Recharging:** 5 Investiture or 5 gems per charge
- **Enhanced Attributes:** Strength +2, Speed +2 while worn
- **Invested Ability Protection:** Disadvantage on your Invested ability tests; Invested ability tests against you gain disadvantage; deflect applies to all damage types

When out of charges: gains Cumbersome [6], loses Enhanced Attributes and Invested Ability Protection.

**Radiant Shardplate Additional Traits:**
- **Living Armor:** Summon or dismiss armor pieces as ▷ (maintains charge count)
- **Restraining Armor:** Can summon around a willing, unaware, or Surprised target within 20 ft. (Discipline vs. Physical for unwilling). While restrained in it: all benefits of Shardplate, but Restrained
- **Natural Recovery:** Regains all charges after a long rest
- No Dangerous trait; no Invested ability test disadvantage

## Fabrials

Fabrials are Stormlight-powered devices made by trapping a spren in a gemstone surrounded by metal latticework.

**Activating:** Use **Interact (▶)** while touching the fabrial. Expends 1 charge; continues expending charges until deactivated (**touch as ▷**) or out of charges.

## Crafting

### Crafting Mundane Items
**Prerequisites:** Relevant expertise (e.g., Weapon Crafting or Sidesword expertise to craft a sidesword)

**Steps:**
1. Acquire materials (cost = half item price)
2. Make a Crafting test (raise the stakes)
3. Apply upgrades and drawbacks

**Time:** Produce ~100 marks worth of work per workday. Each Opportunity reduces time by 25%; each Complication increases it by 25%.

### Crafting Test Results

| Result | Outcome |
|--------|---------|
| 1–5 | Utter Failure — nothing usable; start over |
| 6–10 | Shoddy: item + no upgrades + 1 drawback |
| 11–20 | Typical: item + 1 upgrade + 1 drawback |
| 21–25 | Quality: item + 2 upgrades + 1 drawback |
| 26+ | Exceptional: item + 3 upgrades + no drawbacks |

### Item Upgrades and Drawbacks (d6)

| d6 | Upgrade | Drawback |
|----|---------|---------|
| 1 | No material cost | Double material cost |
| 2 | Earn crafting acclaim | Damage tools/facilities |
| 3 | Half weight | Double weight |
| 4 | Sturdy (ignore Complication damage) | Delicate (Complication can break it) |
| 5 | Impressively beautiful | Eyesore |
| 6 | Attracts a potential patron | Makes scholars/ardents jealous |

### Advanced Item Features (spend 2 upgrades for 1 feature)

| Feature | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Adorned | Armor gains Presentable as expert trait |

## Inventing Unique Fabrials

Unique fabrials produce effects that standard production cannot — including mimicking Radiant surges.

**Prerequisites:** Access to a specially cut gemstone (earned as a reward; tier matches character tier).

**Steps:**
1. Choose desired effect from the Unique Fabrial Effects table
2. Acquire materials (tier 1: 100 mk; tier 2: 200 mk; tier 3: 400 mk; tier 4: 800 mk)
3. Trap a spren (Lore test: tier 1 DC 15 → tier 4 DC 30; 1 day per attempt)
4. Make a Crafting test (1 day per tier of effect; see Fabrial Crafting Test results)
5. Apply upgrades and drawbacks

### Selected Unique Fabrial Descriptions

**Accelerator (T2):** Move up to your movement rate in a straight line as ▷. DC 15 Agility or fall Prone.  
**Bindrial (area) (T1):** Immobilize everything (but you) within 5 ft. as ▶; 1 charge per turn.  
**Bindrial (self) (T1):** Climb any surface for 1 round as ▷; stays in place without another charge.  
**Cremrial (T1):** Soften a Medium stone area; creatures must Avoid Danger or be Immobilized when it hardens.  
**Disruptor (T3):** Destroy unattended Small objects or attack characters (Agility vs. Physical; 2d6 spirit damage).  
**Drainer (T2):** Drain 2 Investiture instead of 1.  
**Liferial (T2):** Restore 1d6 health; optionally remove one injury (both cost 1 charge).  
**Lightrial (T2):** Deception vs. Cognitive; on success, target loses 3 focus.  
**Surge Fabrial (T4):** Choose one Radiant surge. Use it (and optionally its talents) via this fabrial, using charges as Investiture.
