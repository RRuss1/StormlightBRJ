# Appendix: Animal Companions

## Using Animal Companions

Animal companions can be gained as rewards. Having one meets the prerequisite for the Hunter's Animal Bond talent.

When your companion drops to 0 health, they become **Unconscious** (not dead) but stop fighting. They can be revived during a short rest by rolling their recovery die.

## Customizing Stat Blocks

With GM permission, modify existing stat blocks to represent other animals. For each feature or benefit you add, remove or reduce an existing one.

### Example Features to Add

| Feature | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Aquatic / Climber / Furtive / Spry | Chosen movement type doesn't Slow the animal |
| Carapace / Shell | Deflect +2 |
| Enhanced Senses | Advantage on primary-sense perception tests |
| Evasive / Skyborn / Slippery | No Reactive Strikes during chosen movement type |
| Fleet | One movement rate +10 ft. |
| Flight | Flying rate 30 ft. |
| Hearty | Health +10 |
| Jumper | Jumps twice as far without a test |
| Nimble | Can share spaces; attacks against it gain disadvantage while sharing |
| Skilled | Gain 1–3 additional ranks in a chosen skill (scales with tier) |
| Sleek | Counts as one size smaller when squeezing |

## Special Companion: Larkin

Critically endangered crustaceans that **drain Investiture** from gems, fabrials, and living Radiants. They are associated with the ancient Knights Radiant.

**Gaining a Larkin:** Can be given as a reward at any tier (very rare — unlikely more than one per campaign).

**Growing Your Larkin:** If tier 3+ and you have a larkin companion, pursue the goal "Grow Your Larkin" — journey to the ruins of Aimia, bond a luckspren, and return alive. Reward: your larkin molts into a **greater larkin**.

### Larkin *(Special Companion – Small)*

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Physical Defense | 14 / Deflect: 2 (carapace) |
| Health | 14 (12–16) |
| Focus | 4 / Investiture: 5 |
| Movement | 30 ft. / fly 20 ft. |
| Skills | Agility +5, Insight +4, Perception +5, Survival +4 |

**Features:** Weak Wings (can't fly farther than flying rate per turn even with extra Move).  
**Languages:** Understands all Rosharan languages; can't speak.

**Actions:**
- **Bite** (+5; 1d4+5 keen; on hit, spend 2 focus to use Drain Light — auto-success)
- **Drain Light** (choose target within 5 ft.): Object loses 1d4 charges; group of spheres loses 1d4 infused marks/broams; or character/effect loses 1d4 Investiture. Must succeed Agility vs. Spiritual for characters. Larkin regains drained amount. Can't drain characters in Invested Shardplate.
- **Invested Healing** (spend 1 Investiture): Recover 1d6+2 health

### Greater Larkin *(Special Companion – Small)*

| Stat | Value |
