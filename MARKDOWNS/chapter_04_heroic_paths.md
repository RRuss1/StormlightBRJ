# Chapter 4: Heroic Paths

## Using Paths and Talents

Heroic paths are the source of most PC abilities. Each path starts with a **key talent** that unlocks three specialties. You gain your first talent at character creation and choose additional talents each level.

### Advancing Through a Path

- The **key talent** is always available (meeting prerequisites).
- Deeper talents are accessible once you have the talent directly connected to them in the tree.
- You can follow multiple paths (**multi-pathing**).
- If a talent name appears in multiple paths, you skip the duplicate — you already have it.

### Starting Path Bonus
Your **starting path** grants 1 free rank in its associated skill. Branching to other paths later does NOT grant those paths' starting skills.

### Using Talents Outside Combat
In conversations and endeavors, action-cost talents count as one contribution. Free-action talents can be used at any time.

## Agent — Heroic Path

*A talented operative who solves problems with a keen mind or deft hand.*

**Starting Skill:** Insight  
**Specialties:** Investigator, Spy, Thief

### Building an Agent
- **Attributes:** Awareness, Intellect, Speed
- **Skills:** Agility, Deception, Deduction, Insight, Light Weaponry, Thievery
- **Multi-path combos:** Hunter and Scholar
- **Rewards:** Bodyguard companion, criminal or high society patrons

### Opportunist (Agent Key)
*Prerequisite: none | ★*

Once per round, when you roll a plot die, you can **reroll** that result. The original roll has no effect.

---

### Investigator Specialty

**Watchful Eye ↻** *(Deduction 1+)*  
When a willing ally within 20 ft. rolls a plot die, use your Opportunist on it as if it were your own.

**Get 'Em Talking ▶** *(Insight 2+)*  
Spend 1 focus; test Deduction vs. Spiritual. On success, learn the target's motivation for the scene. Until end of scene, you can raise the stakes on tests that leverage this motivation.

**Quick Analysis ▶▶** *(Watchful Eye)*  
Spend 2 focus to gain ▶▶ usable only for cognitive skill tests via Use a Skill, Gain Advantage, or an Agent talent.

**Gather Evidence ∞** *(Insight 2+, Quick Analysis)*  
After succeeding on a cognitive test against a character, become **Focused** until end of next turn. Gain Legal Codes utility expertise.

**Hardy ∞** *(Baleful)*  
Gain +1 max health per level (including retroactively).

**Baleful ∞** *(Get 'Em Talking)*  
To resist your influence, a character must spend **extra focus equal to your tier**.

**Sleuth's Instincts ∞** *(Deduction 3+, Hardy)*  
Advantage on cognitive tests against characters whose motivation you know. You **automatically know** when those characters lie to you.

**Close the Case ▶▶▶** *(Deduction 3+, Hardy)*  
Once per scene, spend 3 focus; test Deduction vs. Cognitive (advantage if you know their motivation). On failure, they gain advantage against you. On success, they back down.

### Spy Specialty

**Sure Outcome ★** *(Insight 2+)*  
When using Opportunist, instead of rerolling, spend 2 focus to **flip** the die: change a Complication to +4, or any result to Opportunity.

**Plausible Excuse ↻** *(Deception 1+)*  
After being discovered skulking, spend 2 focus to feign innocence — you influence everyone who can sense you. Resisting costs extra focus equal to your Deception ranks. Gain **Sleight of Hand** expertise.

**Collected ∞** *(Sure Outcome)*  
Cognitive and Spiritual defenses each **+2**.

**Cover Story ∞** *(Plausible Excuse)*  
Gain a false identity and a relevant cultural expertise.

**Subtle Takedown ▶▶** *(Insight 3+, Cover Story)*  
Make an unarmed attack using Insight vs. Cognitive of a Surprised/unsensing/non-threatening target. On a hit, they can't communicate until end of their next turn. Spend Opportunity to auto-succeed Grapple and knock them Prone.

**Mercurial Facade ★** *(Deception 3+, Subtle Takedown)*  
Disguise yourself using Deception with no physical supplies needed. The first character to see through your disguise becomes **Surprised**.

**High Society Contacts ▶** *(Patron in high society, Cover Story)*  
Spend 2 focus to add an Opportunity to any test in high society. Gain **High Society** cultural expertise.

**Mighty ▶** *(Cover Story)*  
When you hit with a weapon or unarmed attack, for each ▶ spent on the attack, deal extra damage equal to **1 + your tier**.

---

### Thief Specialty

**Risky Behavior ▶** *(Insight 2+)*  
Before making a test, spend 1 focus to **raise the stakes**.

**Cheap Shot ▶** *(Opportunist key)*  
While hand free, spend 1 focus; make an unarmed attack using Thievery vs. Cognitive (raise stakes). On hit, target is **Stunned**. On hit or graze, spend Opportunity to steal one item.

**Double Down ★** *(Risky Behavior)*  
After using Opportunist, reroll one additional time. You must keep the final result; on a Complication, you lose 2 focus.

**Surefooted ∞** *(Cheap Shot)*  
Movement +10. Reduce damage from falling/dangerous terrain by **2 × tier**.

**Shadow Step ↻** *(Thievery 3+, Surefooted)*  
After Disengaging, spend 2 focus; test Thievery vs. each nearby enemy's Cognitive to hide (advantage if in cover/obscured).

**Trickster's Hand ▶▶** *(Thievery 3+, Surefooted)*  
Spend 2 focus to gain ▶▶ usable only for physical skill tests via Use a Skill, Gain Advantage, or Agent talents.

**Fast Talker ▶▶** *(Insight 3+, Double Down)*  
Spend 2 focus to gain ▶▶ usable only for spiritual skill tests via Use a Skill, Gain Advantage, or Agent talents.

**Underworld Contacts ▶** *(Patron/follower in criminal underworld, Double Down)*  
Spend 2 focus to add an Opportunity to social tests against criminals. Gain **Criminal Groups** expertise.

## Envoy — Heroic Path

*An insightful negotiator who adeptly influences others.*

**Starting Skill:** Discipline  
**Specialties:** Diplomat, Faithful, Mentor

### Building an Envoy
- **Attributes:** Presence, Willpower
- **Skills:** Discipline, Deception, Leadership, Lore, Persuasion
- **Multi-path combos:** Leader and Scholar
- **Rewards:** Influential patrons

### Rousing Presence (Envoy Key)
*Prerequisite: none | ▶*

Choose an ally you can influence. They become **Determined** until they benefit from it or the scene ends.

---

**Steadfast Challenge ▶** *(Discipline 1+)*

**Well Dressed ∞** *(Steadfast Challenge)*  
While wearing Presentable armor or fashionable clothing, gain advantage on first Deception, Leadership, or Persuasion test each scene. Gain **Fashion** expertise.

**Collected ∞** *(Rousing Presence key)*  
Cognitive and Spiritual defenses each +2.

**Withering Retort ↻** *(Discipline 2+, Steadfast Challenge)*  
Before a character targets you with an attack, use Steadfast Challenge as ↻ and add your Discipline ranks to your deflect against that attack.

**Calm Appeal ▶** *(Discipline 2+, Withering Retort)*  
When Steadfast Challenge Disorients a target, spend 1 focus to also pacify them (no attacks without provocation). Resisting costs extra focus equal to your Discipline ranks.

**High Society Contacts ▶** *(Patron in high society, Well Dressed)*  
Same as Agent version. Gain **High Society** expertise.

**Practiced Oratory ▶/▶▶** *(Persuasion 3+, High Society Contacts)*  
When using Rousing Presence or Steadfast Challenge, spend focus up to your Persuasion ranks to affect that many **additional targets**.

**Peaceful Solution ▶** *(Discipline 3+, Calm Appeal)*  
If all non-minion enemies are currently pacified and all characters agree to cease hostilities, immediately **end combat** and transition to a conversation.

**Stalwart Presence ▶** *(Discipline 2+, Devoted Presence)*

**Applied Motivation ↻** *(Discipline 2+, Galvanize)*

**Inspired Zeal ↻** *(Discipline 3+, Applied Motivation or Stalwart Presence)*

**Sage Counsel ↻** *(Lore 3+, Stalwart Presence)*  
After you Aid a character, spend 1 focus to also grant them the benefit of **Rousing Presence** for free.

**Rallying Shout ▶** *(Leadership 3+, Guiding Oration)*

## Hunter — Heroic Path

*A skilled sharpshooter and outdoorsperson who seeks and eliminates problems.*

**Starting Skill:** Perception  
**Specialties:** Archer, Assassin, Tracker

### Building a Hunter
- **Attributes:** Awareness, Strength, Speed
- **Skills:** Agility, Perception, Stealth, Survival, Light or Heavy Weaponry
- **Multi-path combos:** Warrior and Agent
- **Rewards:** Animal companion, grandbow, Shardblade

### Seek Quarry (Hunter Key)
*Prerequisite: none | ★ (1 minute preparation)*

Choose a character you can sense or have previously encountered as your **quarry**. You gain **advantage on all tests to find, attack, or study your quarry**.

---

### Archer Specialty

**Combat Training ∞** *(Seek Quarry key)*  
Once per round on a miss, graze one target without spending focus. Gain one weapon expertise, one armor expertise, and **Military Life** cultural expertise.

**Tagging Shot ▶** *(Perception 2+)*  
Move 5 ft. and make a ranged attack. On hit or graze, make the target your quarry.

**Sharp Eye ▶** *(Tagging Shot)*  
After observing quarry for ▶ (or any character for 1 minute), test Perception vs. Cognitive. On success, learn: lowest attribute, lowest defense, or whether health/focus/Investiture is below half.

**Steady Aim ▶** *(Agility 1+, Combat Training)*  
Until end of turn, both ranges increase by half. On a hit with a ranged weapon, deal extra damage equal to your Perception ranks.

**Backstep ↻** *(Agility 2+, Steady Aim)*  
After a ranged attack, spend 2 focus to Disengage as ↻. If you end in cover or obscured, gain the Brace benefit.

**Hardy ∞** *(Steady Aim)*  
+1 max health per level (retroactive).

**Exploit Weakness ▶** *(Perception 3+, Sharp Eye)*  
Use Gain Advantage as ▶ targeting only your quarry (no ▶▶ cost).

**Unrelenting Salvo ▶** *(Agility 3+, Hardy)*  
You can use the same ranged weapon to **Strike your quarry more than once per turn** (not limited to one Strike per hand).

---

### Assassin Specialty

**Startling Blow ▶** *(Stealth 1+)*  
Make an unarmed or improvised attack vs. Cognitive of a target your size or smaller. On hit or graze, target becomes **Surprised** until end of your next turn.

**Killing Edge ∞** *(Perception 2+)*  
Gain Knives and Slings expertises. While wielding them, these weapons gain **Deadly** and **Quickdraw**.

**Cold Eyes ↻** *(Shadowing)*  
After killing/incapacitating your quarry, recover 1 focus and **designate a new quarry**.

**Swift Strikes ▶** *(Cold Eyes)*  
Spend 1 focus to make a **second Strike** with the same hand.

**Fatal Thrust ▶▶** *(Perception 3+, Startling Blow)*  
Attack a Surprised/unaware/unsuspecting target (advantage if weapon is Discreet) using a light weapon vs. Cognitive. Add **4d4 extra damage** (scales: 6d4 at tier 3, 8d4 at tier 4, 10d4 at tier 5). Max damage die rolls apply a penalty to target's injury roll.

**Shadowing ∞** *(Stealth 2+, Killing Edge)*  
Quarry gains disadvantage to sense you; you gain advantage to avoid their notice. Succeed vs. Spiritual while in cover/obscured → spend 3 focus to make the target your quarry.

**Mighty ▶** *(Cold Eyes)*  
Standard Mighty talent.

**Sidestep ∞** *(Stealth 3+, Shadowing)*  
Gain an additional ↻ at start of combat and each turn, **usable only for Dodge**. Unavailable while wearing armor with deflect 2+.

---

### Tracker Specialty

**Deadly Trap ▶▶** *(Survival 1+)*  
Create and conceal a trap within reach. Choose:  
- **Entangling (Survival vs. Cognitive):** 2d4 impact, target Immobilized 1 round, area becomes difficult terrain.  
- **Impaling (Survival vs. Physical):** 2d4 keen, target Afflicted [vital = 3 + Survival ranks], spend ▶ to inflict an injury.  
Max active traps = your Survival ranks. Search/disarm DC = your Spiritual defense.

**Animal Bond ★** *(Have animal companion)*  
Upgrade an animal companion reward. Can communicate simple concepts. Alert to incoming danger (defenses +1 while within 10 ft.). Can give companion ▶ or ▶▶ during your turn. Special **Track** action: companion designates a target as your quarry.

**Experienced Trapper ∞** *(Perception 2+, Deadly Trap)*  
Foraging no longer uses your full short rest time. Auto-succeed on foraging Survival (if resources exist). Sustain a number of characters = your Survival ranks. Trap damage → 2d6, duration → 2 rounds. Craft natural tools with Survival.

**Surefooted ∞** *(Experienced Trapper)*  
Movement +10. Reduce dangerous terrain/falling damage by 2 × tier.

**Protective Bond ▶** *(Animal Bond)*  
Assign your companion to protect an ally within 30 ft.; ally gains Animal Bond defense bonus instead.

**Feral Connection ∞** *(Survival 2+, Protective Bond)*  
Companion health +5 × tier (scales); defenses +2; tests gain bonus = your Survival ranks. Gain **Animal Care** expertise.

**Pack Hunting ↻** *(Perception 3+, Protective Bond)*  
When an ally attacks your quarry, spend 1 focus to add your **Survival ranks** to their attack test or damage roll.

**Hunter's Edge ∞** *(Survival 3+, Experienced Trapper)*  
Companion gains advantage against your quarry. Trap damage → 2d8, duration → 3 rounds.

## Leader — Heroic Path

*A poised commander who directs and guides others to be their best.*

**Starting Skill:** Leadership

### Building a Leader
- **Attributes:** Presence, Strength, Willpower
- **Skills:** Athletics, Deception, Heavy Weaponry, Intimidation, Leadership, Persuasion
- **Multi-path combos:** Warrior and Envoy

### Decisive Command (Leader Key)
*Prerequisite: none | ▶*

Gain a **command die** (starts as d4). Spend 1 focus to give an ally within 20 ft. your command die. Before the end of their next turn, they can roll it with any one test and add it to any single die in that roll (not the plot die).

---

+1 max health per level (retroactive).

**Resolute Stand ▶** *(Athletics 1+, Hardy)*  
Spend focus = your Leadership ranks to target that many additional characters with Valiant Intervention. Affected targets can't make Reactive Strikes against your allies.

**Demonstrative Command ★** *(Leadership 2+, Resolute Stand)*  
Spend 1 focus to roll your command die and add it to your Athletics, Agility, or Leadership d20 roll. Command die size increases by one step.

**Resilient Hero ↻** *(Athletics 3+, Resolute Stand)*  
Once per scene, before your health drops to 0, instead set it to your **Athletics modifier**.

**Mighty ▶** *(Imposing Posture)*  
Standard Mighty talent.

## Scholar — Heroic Path

*An adroit thinker who excels at planning and building.*

**Starting Skill:** Lore  
**Specialties:** Artifabrian, Strategist, Surgeon

### Building a Scholar
- **Attributes:** Intellect above all, then Presence + Speed or Strength
- **Skills:** Crafting, Deduction, Lore, Medicine
- **Multi-path combos:** Agent and Envoy
- **Rewards:** Fabrials, mentor companion, Soulcaster

### Erudition (Scholar Key)
*Prerequisite: none | ★*

Choose one cultural or utility expertise you don't have, and two different non-surge cognitive skills. You **count as having** the expertise and one extra rank in each chosen skill (even above your normal maximum). These don't count toward prerequisites and are temporary.

After a long rest with library access, you can **reassign** these.

---

### Artifabrian Specialty

**Efficient Engineer ∞** *(Crafting 1+)*  
Gain a crafting expertise (Armor, Equipment, or Weapon). When crafting or inventing fabrials: Opportunity range expands by 2; material costs halved. Gain one bonus fabrial (choose from a small selection).

**Prized Acquisition ∞** *(Erudition key)*  
Gain **Fabrial Crafting** specialist expertise. Gain a specially cut gemstone to craft a tier-matching unique fabrial. The first craft ignores normal time requirements. You cannot sell this gem; it can be salvaged from a fabrial you dismantle.

**Fine Handiwork ★** *(Efficient Engineer)*  
When crafting, advanced features cost **one upgrade** instead of two (once per item).

**Inventive Design ★** *(Crafting 2+, Prized Acquisition)*  
When crafting a fabrial using your Prized Acquisition gem, you can select an effect **one tier higher** than your current tier.

**Overcharge ▶** *(Crafting 3+, Prized Acquisition)*  
Once per turn, raise the stakes on a fabrial attack test. Spend Opportunity to also Strike with that fabrial as ▶ on the same turn. GM can spend Complication to add a drawback to the fabrial.

**Deep Study ∞** *(Efficient Engineer)*  
Erudition grants one additional expertise and two additional non-surge cognitive skills.

**Experimental Tinkering ★** *(Fine Handiwork)*  
Opportunity range expands by 1 more; crafting time halved. During a long rest, attempt to reconfigure your Prized Acquisition fabrial into a different fabrial (same tier or lower) without new materials.

**Overwhelm with Details ▶▶** *(Lore 3+, Experimental Tinkering)*  
Spend 2 focus to use your **Lore modifier** instead of the usual skill modifier on a cognitive or spiritual test.

---

### Strategist Specialty

**Strategize ↻** *(Deduction 1+, Erudition key)*  
After succeeding on Gain Advantage using an Erudition skill, give the advantage to an **ally** instead. Optionally spend 2 focus to prevent the target from using reactions against that ally.

**Mind and Body ∞** *(Erudition key)*  
Erudition grants one additional skill and can now include **physical skills** (not surges). Gain one weapon expertise.

**Composed ∞** *(Strategize)*  
Max and current focus increase by your tier. Scales with tier.

**Know Your Moment ∞** *(Deduction 2+, Mind and Body)*  
After each round begins, all your defenses increase by 2 until the start of your turn.

**Deep Contemplation ★** *(Lore 2+, Composed)*  
Reassign up to 2 skills and expertises from Erudition.

**Keen Insight ↻** *(Deep Contemplation)*  
After you succeed on Gain Advantage, the target must resist your influence or gain **disadvantage on their next test** during this encounter.

**Contingency ↻** *(Lore 3+, Composed)*  
After an ally within 20 ft. rolls a Complication, spend 2 focus to **remove it** from their test.

**Turning Point ▶▶** *(Deduction 3+, Contingency)*  
Once per scene, spend 2 focus; test Deduction vs. enemy leader's Cognitive (advantage if you took a slow turn). On success, you and your allies each gain **▶ on your next turns**.

---

### Surgeon Specialty

**Field Medicine ▶▶** *(Medicine 1+, Erudition key)*  
Spend 1 focus; test Medicine DC 15 to treat a conscious ally within reach. Disadvantage if treating yourself. On success: they recover recovery die + your Medicine ranks in health. On failure: recovery die only.

**Emotional Intelligence ∞** *(Erudition key)*  
Erudition grants one additional skill and can include **spiritual skills** (not surges). Gain **Diagnosis** utility expertise.

**Anatomical Insight ▶** *(Field Medicine)*  
When you hit a target your size or smaller with an unarmed attack, spend 1 focus or ▶ to apply **Exhausted [−half your Medicine ranks]**.

**Collected ∞** *(Emotional Intelligence)*  
Cognitive and Spiritual defenses each +2.

**Swift Healer ∞** *(Medicine 2+, Field Medicine)*  
Use Field Medicine as ▶ instead of ▶▶. All your healing abilities restore additional health equal to your **Medicine ranks**.

**Applied Medicine ∞** *(Lore 2+, Collected)*  
When you cause a character to recover health, they recover additional health equal to your **Lore ranks**.

**Ongoing Care ★** *(Lore 3+, Swift Healer)*  
Forgo a short or long rest to treat an ally within reach. After rest, test DC 10 Medicine (+5 per injury beyond the first). On success, remove one condition caused by an injury. Gain **Mental Health Care** expertise.

**Resuscitation ▶▶▶** *(Medicine 3+, Swift Healer)*  
Use Field Medicine to revive an Unconscious or recently dead character (died within Medicine ranks rounds). Spend 3 focus instead of 1. DC increases by 5 per injury beyond the first.

## Warrior — Heroic Path

*A fighter who relies on their skill, brute strength, or indomitable will.*

**Starting Skill:** Athletics  
**Specialties:** Duelist, Shardbearer, Soldier

### Building a Warrior
- **Attributes:** Speed, Strength, then Awareness and Willpower
- **Skills:** Athletics, Light Weaponry, Heavy Weaponry, Intimidation, Leadership, Persuasion
- **Multi-path combos:** Leader and Hunter
- **Rewards:** Shardblade, Shardplate, military title

### Vigilant Stance (Warrior Key)
*Prerequisite: none | ★*

You learn to use **stances**. You begin with Vigilant Stance:

> **Vigilant Stance:** Reduce focus cost of Dodge and Reactive Strike by 1. You can enter another known stance as ▷.

You can enter a known stance as the action specified by that stance's talent. Stances end when you end them as ▷, enter a new stance, or the scene ends. By default, stances are usable only in combat.

---

### Duelist Specialty

**Flamestance ▶** *(Intimidation 1+)*  
New stance: While in Flamestance, gain advantage on Intimidation. If exactly one enemy is in your reach and no allies are within that enemy's reach, gain ▶ usable only for Gain Advantage or attacks targeting that enemy.

**Practiced Kata ∞** *(Vigilant Stance key)*  
Use fighting stances in conversations and endeavors. Activating/changing a stance in those scenes costs 1 focus and counts as your contribution. At scene start (if not Surprised), enter Vigilant Stance for free.

**Signature Weapon ∞** *(Flamestance)*  
Gain a weapon expertise. Choose one weapon expertise: its Opportunity range expands by 1 (by 2 at tier 3).

**Feinting Strike ▶▶** *(Intimidation 2+, Flamestance)*  
Spend 2 focus; melee weapon attack vs. Cognitive. On hit: target loses one ▶ and focus equal to your Intimidation ranks. On graze: half focus, no ▶ loss. Spend Opportunity to gain ▶ for Strike or activating a stance.

**Ironstance ▶** *(Athletics 2+, Practiced Kata)*  
New stance: Gain advantage on Insight. When a character in your reach misses or grazes you, use Reactive Strike against them as if they voluntarily left your reach.

**Surefooted ∞** *(Ironstance)*  
Movement +10. Reduce dangerous terrain/falling damage by 2 × tier.

**Vinestance ▶** *(Athletics 3+, Feinting Strike)*  
New stance: Physical and Cognitive defenses +1. After being hit or grazed in melee, spend ▶ to test Athletics vs. attacker's Cognitive. On success: target loses 1d4 focus and is pushed up to 5 ft. × your Athletics ranks.

**Wit's End ▶** *(Intimidation 3+, Feinting Strike)*  
Spend 1 focus; move up to half movement; melee attack vs. Cognitive of a target with **0 focus**. This attack ignores deflect and deals +4d6 damage (scales: 6d6 at tier 3, 8d6 at tier 4, 10d6 at tier 5). Can't graze.

---

### Shardbearer Specialty

**Windstance ▶** *(Perception 1+, Shard Training)*

**Mighty ▶** *(Stonestance)*  
Standard Mighty talent.

**Shattering Blow ▶** *(Perception 2+, Windstance)*  
When you hit with a melee attack (two-handed or hand free), spend 2 focus: target's armor loses 1 charge. Each target that lost health is pushed 5 ft. away (+5 ft. per Strength above 5). If pushed into a solid object, target takes falling-equivalent damage.

**Bloodstance ▶** *(Athletics 2+, Mighty or Shard Training)*  
New stance: Opportunity range for attacks and physical tests expands by 2, but Physical, Cognitive, and Spiritual defenses each decrease by 2.

**Precise Parry ↻** *(Perception 3+, Shattering Blow)*  
Before being hit by a melee attack, spend 1 focus to attempt to parry:  
- **Unarmed (disadvantage unless Shardblade):** Test Athletics; spend Opportunity to disarm opponent.  
- **Wielding weapon:** Test Light or Heavy Weaponry; spend Opportunity to destroy non-Invested weapon (if you wield a Shardblade).  
DC = triggering attack result. On success: hit becomes a graze.

**Meteoric Leap ▶▶** *(Athletics 3+, Bloodstance)*  
Spend 2 focus; leap up to quarter movement; make an unarmed attack vs. Physical of targets within reach. Roll double damage dice. Targets with lower Strength than you are knocked Prone. Advantage while wearing Shardplate.

### Soldier Specialty

**Cautious Advance ▶** *(Discipline 1+)*  
Move up to half movement, ignoring difficult terrain. Gain ▶ usable only for Brace or Gain Advantage.

**Combat Training ∞** *(Vigilant Stance key)*  
Same as Hunter's Combat Training.

**Defensive Position ∞** *(Athletics 2+, Cautious Advance)*  
Brace adds **two disadvantages** to attacks against you instead of one. Allies within 5 ft. can Brace as if behind your shield.

**Formation Drills ∞** *(Discipline 2+, Defensive Position)*  
Allies within 10 ft. who Brace also gain the benefit of your Defensive Position.

**Hardy ∞** *(Combat Training)*  
+1 max health per level (retroactive).

**Swift Strikes ▶** *(Hardy)*  
Spend 1 focus to make a **second Strike** with the same hand.

**Devastating Blow ▶▶** *(Athletics 3+, Combat Training)*  
Make a melee weapon attack vs. Physical. Add **extra 2d8 damage** (scales: 3d8 at tier 3, 4d8 at tier 4, 5d8 at tier 5).

**Wary ∞** *(Discipline 3+, Defensive Position)*  
While you have 1+ focus, you **can't be Surprised**. When you involuntarily lose focus, reduce the amount by your Discipline ranks (minimum 1 lost).
